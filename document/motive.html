Motivation

Compilation of the "Atlas of Gamma-rays from the Inelastic Scattering of Reactor Fast Neutrons" (1978DE41) by
A.M. Demidov, L.I. Govor, Yu.K. Cherepantsev, M.R. Ahmed, S. Al-Najjar, M.A. Al-Amili, N. Al-Assafi, and N. Rammo

Reference paper: arXiv:2001.11140v2 [physics.app-ph]

The above reference paper hosted on the arXiv describes the motivation behind compiling and disseminating the data in the Baghdad Atlas, along its utility as a relational database. An older version of the reference paper is also available: LBNL Report Number LBNL-1007259
